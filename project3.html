Catcall Control

Custom Video Game and Controller

This 7 week project was about building our own interactive game to inspire change in a social cause. We were tasked with creating our own interactive video game and physical controllers. We wanted to weave strong storytelling into our game to create empathy and education about the harmful nature of catcalling while also emphasizing fun and interactivity.
